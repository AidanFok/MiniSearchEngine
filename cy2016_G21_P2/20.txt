scene iii  london  before the tower 


enter gloucester  with his serving men in blue coats


gloucester

i am come to survey the tower this day 
since henry death  i fear  there is conveyance 
where be these warders  that they wait not here 
open the gates   gloucester that calls 


first warder

 within   who there that knocks so imperiously 
first serving man	it is the noble duke of gloucester 


second warder

 within   whoe he be  you may not be let in 
first serving man	villains  answer you so the lord protector 


first warder

 within   the lord protect him  so we answer him 
we do no otherwise than we are will d 


gloucester

who willed you  or whose will stands but mine 
there none protector of the realm but i 
break up the gates  i be your warrantize 
shall i be flouted thus by dunghill grooms 
gloucester men rush at the tower gates  and woodvile the lieutenant speaks within


woodvile

what noise is this  what traitors have we here 


gloucester

lieutenant  is it you whose voice i hear 
open the gates  here gloucester that would enter 


woodvile

have patience  noble duke  i may not open 
the cardinal of winchester forbids 
from him i have express commandment
that thou nor none of thine shall be let in 


gloucester

faint hearted woodvile  prizest him  me 
arrogant winchester  that haughty prelate 
whom henry  our late sovereign  ne could brook 
thou art no friend to god or to the king 
open the gates  or i shut thee out shortly 
serving men	open the gates unto the lord protector 
or we burst them open  if that you come not quickly 
enter to the protector at the tower gates bishop of winchester and his men in tawny coats
bishop


of winchester

how now  ambitious humphry  what means this 


gloucester

peel priest  dost thou command me to be shut out 
bishop


of winchester

i do  thou most usurping proditor 
and not protector  of the king or realm 


gloucester

stand back  thou manifest conspirator 
thou that contrivedst to murder our dead lord 
thou that givest whores indulgences to sin 
i canvass thee in thy broad cardinal hat 
if thou proceed in this thy insolence 
bishop


of winchester

nay  stand thou back  i will not budge a foot 
this be damascus  be thou cursed cain 
to slay thy brother abel  if thou wilt 


gloucester

i will not slay thee  but i drive thee back 
thy scarlet robes as a child bearing cloth
i use to carry thee out of this place 
bishop


of winchester

do what thou darest  i beard thee to thy face 


gloucester

what  am i dared and bearded to my face 
draw  men  for all this privileged place 
blue coats to tawny coats  priest  beware your beard 
i mean to tug it and to cuff you soundly 
under my feet i stamp thy cardinal hat 
in spite of pope or dignities of church 
here by the cheeks i drag thee up and down 
bishop


of winchester

gloucester  thou wilt answer this before the pope 


gloucester

winchester goose  i cry  a rope  a rope 
now beat them hence  why do you let them stay 
thee i chase hence  thou wolf in sheep array 
out  tawny coats  out  scarlet hypocrite 
here gloucester men beat out bishop of  winchester men  and enter in the hurly  burly the mayor of london and his officers


mayor

fie  lords  that you  being supreme magistrates 
thus contumeliously should break the peace 


gloucester

peace  mayor  thou know little of my wrongs 
here beaufort  that regards nor god nor king 
hath here distrain the tower to his use 
bishop


of winchester

here gloucester  a foe to citizens 
one that still motions war and never peace 
o your free purses with large fines 
that seeks to overthrow religion 
because he is protector of the realm 
and would have armour here out of the tower 
to crown himself king and suppress the prince 


gloucester

i will not answer thee with words  but blows 
here they skirmish again


mayor

naught rests for me in this tumultuous strife
but to make open proclamation 
come  officer  as loud as e thou canst 
cry 


officer

all manner of men assembled here in arms this day
against god peace and the king s  we charge and
command you  in his highness  name  to repair to
your several dwelling places  and not to wear 
handle  or use any sword  weapon  or dagger 
henceforward  upon pain of death 


gloucester

cardinal  i be no breaker of the law 
but we shall meet  and break our minds at large 
bishop


of winchester

gloucester  we will meet  to thy cost  be sure 
thy heart blood i will have for this day work 


mayor

i call for clubs  if you will not away 
this cardinal more haughty than the devil 


gloucester

mayor  farewell  thou dost but what thou mayst 
bishop


of winchester

abominable gloucester  guard thy head 
for i intend to have it ere long 
exeunt  severally  gloucester and bishop of winchester with their serving men


mayor

see the coast clear d  and then we will depart 
good god  these nobles should such stomachs bear 
i myself fight not once in forty year 
exeunt
