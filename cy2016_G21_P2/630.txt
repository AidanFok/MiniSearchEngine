scene iv  the same 


enter exton and servant


exton

didst thou not mark the king  what words he spake 
 i no friend will rid me of this living fear 
was it not so 


servant

                  these were his very words 


exton

 i no friend  quoth he  he spake it twice 
and urged it twice together  did he not 


servant

he did 


exton

and speaking it  he wistly look on me 
and who should say   would thou wert the man 
that would divorce this terror from my heart 
meaning the king at pomfret  come  let go 
i am the king friend  and will rid his foe 
exeunt
